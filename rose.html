Overview

Roseksis/Rosexis (both spellings used interchangeably) and Flyxans are space-dwelling, extremely long-lived creatures who exist in almost perfect peace, nor ailed by plague, scarcity, politics and greed; their burdens instead compose the preservation of other life, seeking understanding of the young universe, creating their own comforts, tools, and arts.

IndexNumber System

Roseksis and their kin use both a trinary and binary *based* number system- meaning the bases used are based on multiples of the former. Trinary isn't used much, and only has the common bases of 3 and 6. Binary is used much, much more, and has 4, 8, and 16 as common bases.

IndexUnits of Measurement

Our most important unit of measurement is that of [relative/subjective/local] time - for Roseksis, they use Suveals (singular, Suve), based on the orbital period of a point on their home ice ring, found by an average altitude of the ring's segments. The ring segments are rather pointless to explicitly list, as they are entirely transient due to their proximity to Suve, and the gravitational and kinetic turmoil involved in such position. A table of positions in the ring follows, the innermost, average altitude point, and outermost sections respectively.

Now with a measurement of time (1.0 suve = 3.9 seconds | 1 second = 0.25 suveals), we can determine distance using the speed of light in a vacuum. One light-suveal is equal to 1 172 500 384 metres.
